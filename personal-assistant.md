### ğŸ¼ Technique Reminders

**Fundamentals**:
- **Breath**: Deep, relaxed breaths from the diaphragm
- **Embouchure**: Firm corners, relaxed center, minimal pressure
- **Tongue**: "Ta" or "Da" articulation, keep it relaxed
- **Posture**: Upright, shoulders relaxed, horn up (not head down)

**Range Development**:
- High register: Fast air, firm corners, minimal pressure
- Low register: Slower air, relaxed embouchure, open throat
- Pedal tones for flexibility and embouchure strength

**Endurance**:
- Build gradually, don't force it
- Rest as much as you play during practice
- Stop before fatigue leads to bad habits
